https://dcmi.github.io/dcap/shex_lite/micro-spec.html

1. A Schema has 
   * zero or one 'start' (a shapeExpr)
   * zero or one array, 'shapes' (consisting of one or more shapeExpr)

2. A shapeExpr is either
   * a NodeConstraint or 
   * a Shape

3. A shapeExprLabel is 
   * an IRI reference

4. A NodeConstraint has
   * exactly one 'id' (a shapeExprLabel)
   * zero or one 'nodeKind' ("iri", "bnode", "nonliteral", or "literal")
   * zero or one 'datatype' (an IRI reference)
   * zero or more 'numericFacet'
   * zero or one array, 'values' (consisting of one or more valueSetValue)

5. A numericFacet is one of:
   * 'mininclusive' (a numericaLiteral) or
   * 'minexclusive' (a numericaLiteral) or
   * 'maxinclusive' (a numericaLiteral) or
   * 'maxexclusive' (a numericaLiteral)

6. A numericLiteral is either 
   * an integer or
   * a decimal number or
   * a float

7. A valueSetValue is either 
   * an objectValue or 
   * an IriStem

8. An objectValue is either
   * an IRI reference or
   * an ObjectLiteral

9. An ObjectLiteral has 
   * exactly one 'value' (a string)
   * zero or one 'language' (a string)
   * zero or one 'type' (a string)

10. An IriStem has
   * exactly one 'stem' (an IRI reference)

11. A Shape has
   * exactly one 'id' (a shapeExprLabel)
   * exactly one 'expression' (a tripleExpr)

12. A tripleExpr is either 
    * exactly one EachOf or 
    * exactly one TripleConstraint

13. An EachOf has 
    * exactly one array, 'expressions' (consisting of one or more tripleExpr) - error?

14. A TripleConstraint has  
    * exactly one 'predicate' (an IRI reference)
    * zero or one 'valueExpr' (a shapeExpr)
    * zero or one 'min' (an integer)
    * zero or one 'max' (an integer)
