Based on https://dcmi.github.io/dcap/shex_lite/micro-spec.html
See also latest ShEx spec https://shexspec.github.io/spec/
JSON-LD context: http://www.w3.org/ns/shex.jsonld
Namespace https://www.w3.org/ns/shex#

1. A Schema has 
   * zero or one array, 'shapes' (consisting of one or more shapeExpr)

2. A shapeExpr is either
   * a NodeConstraint or 
   * a Shape

3. A shapeExprLabel is 
   * an IRI reference

4. A NodeConstraint has
   * exactly one 'id' (a shapeExprLabel)
   * zero or one 'nodeKind' ("iri", "bnode", "nonliteral", or "literal")
   * zero or one 'datatype' (an IRI reference)
   * zero or more 'numericFacet'
   * zero or one array, 'values' (consisting of one or more ObjectValue)

5. A numericFacet is one of:
   * 'mininclusive' (an integer) or
   * 'maxinclusive' (an integer)

8. An ObjectValue is either
   * an IRI reference or
   * an ObjectLiteral

9. An ObjectLiteral has 
   * exactly one 'value' (a string)
   * zero or one 'language' (a string)
   * zero or one 'type' (a string)

11. A Shape has
   * exactly one 'id' (a shapeExprLabel)
   * exactly one 'expression' (a tripleExpr)

12. A tripleExpr is
    * exactly one EachOf

13. An EachOf has 
    * exactly one array, 'expressions' (consisting of one or more TripleConstraint)

14. A TripleConstraint has  
    * exactly one 'predicate' (an IRI reference)
    * zero or one 'valueExpr' (a shapeExpr)
    * zero or one 'min' (an integer)
    * zero or one 'max' (an integer)
